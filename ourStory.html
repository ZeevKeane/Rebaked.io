How it started

On a beautiful 25 of September, 10 000 NFTs were born. They were minted for 42 Ada each and received a warm welcome from the CNFT community. Sadly, their makers were greedy and decided to run with the profits. But the Gingerbread Squad was faster.

What have we done?

We caught them, made them give us the keys to the house and started baking without them. We have no leaders now, we don't need them. The community bakery kept building and building despite being rugged, despite the price of Ada, despite the haters. And we quickly noticed we weren't alone! the CNFT community responded and gave us a warm hug, some kind words and a helping hand to rise stronger than ever. We owe everything to our incredible community and plan to give back as much as we can. By the community, for the community! Vive la revolution.

Christmas 2021 Rebirth:

We will release the first 1000 Christmas themed community NFTs. Majority of these will be gifted to Gingerbread Squad hodlers, so consolidate and delist now! We will take a surprise wallet snapshot. There will also be a minority available to mint at an affordable TBA price. All proceeds will go to the community purse which is public and will be eventually controlled by the DAO.

Q1 2022:

We will have an open beta for the game, with rewards for the most helpful testers. We will also have a closed beta for the "DAO" with Ginger volume based voting. We will also keep releasing community developed NFTs for various holidays and themes. Majority will be gifted and airdropped to community and holders of the first 10 000 Gingerbreads, minority available to mint.

Q2 2022:

Release of public V1 of the game. Rewards for tournaments, missions, and score. Open beta for the DAO, first votes go for game balancing and suggestions for updates. We will also be releasing our original Gingerbread Houses as an NFT for in game use. Continued release of community NFTs on special days. There's one on April we are really looking forward to.

Q3 2022:

We keep giving power to the community! Development, direction and art of the game will begin to be voted by the "DAO", plans for a Gingerbread NFTs market will be discussed and voted, and of course, we will keep releasing our community developed NFTs as airdrops to holders and a small quantity available to mint.

Constant, decentralized, always developing

We are in constant recruitment for any community member who wants to help the project. He, she or they may just be one person, but one person is all that's needed to start something beautiful. Decentralization allows us to be as flexible as water. Every member supports the project in their unique way and we can have as many members as there's people in the world. We can't die, because we have no head, we have no centralized leadership, we are an ever growing and ever evolving community. So if you want to be part of the first fully decentralized NFT project, check our discord. We will also give continuous support towards our individual community members in their personal passions, ambitions and projects. *Disclaimer: "DAO" is shorthand for a voting system heavily based on number of OG Gingers owned by the community member voter. We have no intentions of registering as a legal entity, profit making ventures, or investment into other instruments. We are just a club of art holders that like art, especially Gingerbread based art.
